1/8/2019

Dear Hiring Manager:

The purpose of this letter is to inform you of my desire to be a Library Media Specialist. I believe that I am uniquely qualified for the position considering my education and experience.

The past two summers, I had the privilege of being the Library Media Specialist and Makerspace/Coding Instructor for the Springfield R12 Explore! Program. I enjoyed teaching kindergarten through fifth grade students from all over Southwest Missouri and even some students here visiting from out of state for the summer.

I graduated from the Library Information Science masters program in December 2017, and I have completed teaching certification through an alternative pathways program with the University of Central Missouri. During my graduate program, I gained valuable experience completing 150 practicum hours at the elementary, middle school, and high school levels. I completed 4 weeks of student teaching at Delaware Elementary as a Library Media Specialist and another 12 weeks at Pershing Middle School.
